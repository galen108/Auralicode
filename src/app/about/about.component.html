ABOUT

Auralicode — Built on Listening, Rooted in Service, Driven by Purpose

Auralicode wasn’t created just to build websites.

It was created to serve people. My name is Galen Tenney — Founder, Lead Architect, designer, developer, musician, creative, lifelong Apple enthusiast, and someone who genuinely believes technology should lift people up, not overwhelm them. I live in the Mid-Atlantic region with my wife, Pamela, who is one of my greatest inspirations and supports in this journey.

The word Auralicode begins with “Aural” for a reason:

Before I ever write a line of code, I listen.
  • To your story.
  • To your challenges.
  • To your hopes for what technology could do for you.
  • Because every meaningful solution begins with understanding.

Why I Do This Work

I’ve spent decades working across government, nonprofit, corporate, and creative spaces — and I’ve seen amazing technology, but I’ve also seen systems that feel cold, impersonal, extractive, and disconnected from the people they claim to serve. I believe something better is possible.

Auralicode exists because:

  • I value principles over personality
  • I believe in service as a calling
  • I believe in craftsmanship, care, and integrity
  • I believe technology should be empathetic, ethical, accessible, and human-centered
  • I believe helping others isn’t transactional — it’s meaningful
  • As a follower of Christ, I try to live by:
  • “Love God. Love your neighbor.”
  • And I bring that spirit of compassion, humility, and service into the work I do every day.
  • I am also proudly progressive — believing in fairness, dignity, justice, inclusion, and the idea that technology should lessen burdens, not deepen divides.
